Land of Thirty-Six Forts
             The history of the Chhattisgarh region dates back to about the 4th century ce,
                    when it was known as Southern (or South) Kosala. The name Chhattisgarh, meaning “thirty-six forts,”
                    was formerly applied to the territory of the Haihaya dynasty of Ratanpur, founded about 750.
