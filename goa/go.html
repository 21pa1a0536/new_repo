Pearl of the Orient
             Goa is often referred to as the "Pearl of the Orient."
                    This poetic tag captures the state's picturesque beaches, vibrant nightlife,
                    rich history, and unique blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures.
                    The term "Pearl of the Orient" reflects the beauty and allure
                    of Goa as a destination on the western coast of India.
