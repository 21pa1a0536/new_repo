Land of Coal
             Jharkhand is also sometimes referred to as the "Land of Coal".
                    This tag highlights the state's abundant coal reserves,
                    which are crucial for the country's energy and industrial needs.
                    The presence of extensive coal deposits has made Jharkhand a significant
                    contributor to India's coal production.
