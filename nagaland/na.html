Falcon Capital of the World
             Nagaland is also sometimes called the "Falcon Capital of the World" due to the presence of the Amur Falcon,
                a migratory bird species, which visits the state in large numbers during its annual migration.
                The Amur Falcon conservation efforts in Nagaland have gained attention, and the state has become
                known for its role in the protection of these birds.
